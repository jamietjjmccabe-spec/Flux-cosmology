# Flux/MIP V3/V3.1 Publication Figures

## Figure 1. V3 Model Battle: BIC Ranking
Bar chart of V3 model BIC values. Lower BIC indicates stronger model support after parameter penalty. The null/intercept model remains the absolute winner, while G_sat is the strongest environmental predictor and outperforms mass-only.

## Figure 2. Fixed-Mass Fingerprint
Grouped bar chart comparing Low-F and High-F host environments inside the same host stellar-mass bins. This is the visual test for the "mass is a shadow" claim.

## Figure 3. Distance-Ladder Divergence
Adult-minus-Child H0 proxy offsets across the full sample, calibrators, Hubble-flow, and Hubble-flow redshift/residual subgroups. This visualizes the V3.1 result: the maturity signal is strongest in calibrators and local Hubble-flow, but flattens or reverses in the mid-z survey-dominated regime.
